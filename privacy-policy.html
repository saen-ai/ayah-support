Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Simple Summary: Ayah does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. Your privacy is completely protected.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Ayah, we believe that your relationship with the Holy Quran is deeply personal and private. We have designed our application to respect your privacy completely. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data and privacy.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal data.

Ayah does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Reading history or patterns
  • Bookmarks or preferences
  • Any other personal or behavioral data

Local Storage

All app functionality is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's standard local storage mechanisms:

  • Reading Progress: Stored locally on your device
  • Bookmarks: Stored locally on your device
  • Settings: Theme preferences and font size stored locally
  • Last Read Position: Stored locally on your device

This data never leaves your device. It is not synchronized with any cloud service, not transmitted to our servers (we don't have any servers), and not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Ayah does NOT use:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, no Firebase, nothing)
  • Advertising networks or ad providers
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any other third-party tracking or data collection services

Internet Connection

Ayah works 100% offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function. All Quranic text is embedded within the application at the time of download from the App Store.

Quran Text Source

The Quranic text displayed in Ayah is provided by the Tanzil Project (tanzil.net), a respected source for verified digital Quran text. This text is embedded in the app during development and does not require any network requests.

Children's Privacy

Ayah is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any data whatsoever, there are no privacy concerns for children using the application.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. Your local data is protected by iOS's security features and remains under your control.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data because:

  • All data stays on your device
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • No account or registration is required
  • No data is ever sent to external servers

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this Privacy Policy and the "Last Updated" date above. However, our commitment to not collecting user data will remain unchanged.

Open Source & Transparency

We believe in transparency. If you have technical questions about how the app works or what data it accesses, please feel free to contact us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Ayah app, please contact us:

Email: support@ayahapp.com
Support: ayahapp.com/support

Bottom Line: Ayah collects zero data. Your privacy is 100% protected. Your reading is completely private between you and Allah.
